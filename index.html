About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Electrical Engineering Department with a concurrent role as a Senior Researcher at the Centre of AI and Big Data, at Namal University, Mianwali. My work revolves around teaching, mentoring, and managing research activities, while actively contributing to projects with a focus on industrial collaborations, research and development, and emerging commercialization opportunities.

With a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Riphah International University, my research expertise lies in deep learning and its applications in biomedical engineering, particularly EMG signal analysis. Over the years, I have contributed to numerous peer-reviewed publications and have been part of impactful projects in AI, machine learning, and embedded systems.

My interests include developing AI-driven solutions for healthcare, agriculture, and industrial sectors, with a focus on bridging theoretical advancements and practical implementation. I am committed to fostering innovation through research, development, and strategic partnerships.
